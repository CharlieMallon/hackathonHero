Rhythm Heroes

How to play

Welcome to Rhythm Heroes!
This game is based on dance dance revolution music video game. The goal is to tap the correct arrow when it reaches the target at the bottom of the screen.
To play use the arrow keys ←↑→↓ on desktop and arrows on the bottom of the screen on your mobile. Good luck!
