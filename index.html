Rhythm Dance Game

How to play

Welcome to (Game name)! This game is based on the Dance Dance Revolution arcade game. The goal of this game is to test your rhythm, your musical timing and your reflexes.
First select your song from the song selection. Each song has a different difficulty level labelled under the title so choose wisely!

Once the game starts, you will see directional arrows sliding down your screen at different speeds and intervals according to the rhythm of the song. The goal is to tap the correct arrow when it reaches the target at the bottom of the screen. If your timing is good, your score will increase. If you miss 7 arrows, you lose!

If you are using a keyboard, you can use the arrow keys ←↑→↓ to tap the correct arrow when it is directly over the target arrow on the bottom of the screen. On mobile devices, just tap the correct target at the bottom when the sliding arrow is directly over it. You can control the volume or go back to the home page using the controls at the top of the page. Good luck!
